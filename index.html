UNCHALLENGED REPORMA-ACS EXECOM SLATE DEFENSES POSITIONS IN MDA '23 - '24

Running unopposed, the candidates of Reporma ng mga Mag-aaral ng San Luis-Alliance of Concerned Students (REPORMA-ACS) for the Executive Committee went on stage to face the panel of judges and the crowd for this academic year’s Miting De Avance in Room P700 at Saint Louis University’s Main Campus, yesterday, October 7.
