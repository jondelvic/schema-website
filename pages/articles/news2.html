SAMCIS first-years attend their programs' orientations

Photos by Zhiann Remnor Abendan, Villamor Dumlao, Johan Rickardo Roxas, Jeecel Sanchez, Lord Angelo Trinidad

by Alyanna Lawrice Rasing

August 16, 2023

Share
Tweet

As the new academic year starts, the freshmen of the School of Accountancy, Management, Computing, and Information Studies (SAMCIS) convened for the first day of the Program-Based Freshmen Orientation at the Devesse Audio Visual Room (AVR) today, August 16.

Present during the first day are the students of BS Accountancy and BS Management Accounting for the morning session, and BS Entrepreneurship-BA, BSBA Marketing Management-BA, BSBA Financial Management-BA, BS Hospitality Management, and BS Tourism Management for the afternoon session.

Officer-in-Charge Dean Glory Dela Peña and Mr. Rod Martin de Vera welcomed the students with their warm opening remarks. Associate Dean April Gumnad also appeared on stage and gave the students a virtual tour of the campus facilities, as well as a quick view of the school’s awards, certifications, and accreditations.

Afterward, respective professors and department heads oriented the students on their programs. Members of the co-curricular and extra-curricular organizations of SAMCIS were also given the opportunity to present and promote their own organizations prior to the opening of the membership drive on August 22, Tuesday. Magni Soni Samcis, formerly known as the SAMCIS Chorale, also performed to introduce their newly-established organization.
