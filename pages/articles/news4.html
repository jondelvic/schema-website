Executive Committee calls for '23-'24 KASAMA/SSC 1st Gen Assembly

Photos by Villamor Dumlao and Nicole Alexis Mondiguing

by Axa Gwen Dao-ilang

October 19, 2023

Share
Tweet

Setting off their term productively, the Executive Committee of the Kataas-taasang Sanggunian ng mga Mag-aaral/Supreme Student Council (KASAMA/SSC) called for their first General Assembly yesterday, October 18, at the AVR (Room D-308), Rizal Building, Main Campus.

In attendance were the newly-elected School Assemblies, Congress of Louisians (COL), Commission on Audit (COA), Commission on Elections (COMELEC), and presidents of university-wide organizations, who convened to acquaint themselves, discuss relevant agendas, and review pending and future programs, projects, and activities.

Moderated by the Executive President Ashli Sophia De Castro, the General Assembly tackled the lapses of previous terms of school assemblies and central offices and the visions of their current administration, discussed upcoming activities for October to November, revealed the committees and desks of the Executive Committee, primed attendees for the SLU KASAMA/SSC Training, and noted various suggestions and concerns raised by attendees.

Some of the significant concerns forwarded are related to the expedition of budget proposals, out-of-pocket expenditures of Assembly officers due to budget insufficiency, changes with the Lantern Parade, probable activities for the University Week, and the increased involvement of political organizations in assembly agendas.
