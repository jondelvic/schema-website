SAMCIS seals top seed, ends preliminaries against SEA with perfect record, 5-0

Photos by Glynielle Ezra Casuga, Joshua Daniel Espelita David, Ramon Emmiel Jasmin, Aaliyah Martinez, and Johan Rickardo Roxas

by Ramon Emmiel Jasmin

October 25, 2023

Share
Tweet

The School of Accountancy, Management, Computing, and Information Studies (SAMCIS) secures top seed for the playoffs with a flawless record after outperforming the School of Engineering and Architecture (SEA) in a nail-biter, 93-89, in men’s basketball held at Prince Bernhard Gym, October 24.

The arch-rivals engaged in a relentless exchange of offensive plays as they maintained a neck-and-neck showdown, not letting each other widen the gap right from the jump ball down to the final buzzer.

SEA nearly clinched the match in the last quarter, but SAMCIS showcased an impenetrable defense and executed fast-paced plays that ultimately secured their victory.

SAMCIS couldn't maintain their typical dominance in the game as several players received technical calls and warnings, primarily due to taunting and challenging the calls.

“Mas mag improve pa po kami sa game, at gagawin ko po yung best ko sa semi-finals to championship,” said Zen Carreon, one of SAMCIS’ guards, in preparation for the next round of the men’s basketball.

In the upcoming semi-finals, SAMCIS (1) is set to compete against the School of Nursing, Allied Health, and Biological Sciences (SONAHBS) (4) on October 28.
